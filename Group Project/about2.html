Our Story

Who We Are

Nestled within the bustling heart of Eheliyagoda, DPP Printing stands as a testament to the enduring power of the printed word. More than just a printing shop, DPP embodies a legacy of craftsmanship and innovation, transforming ideas into tangible forms for over 15 years.

The story of DPP begins with our founders' unwavering belief in the power of clear communication. In a world increasingly dominated by digital screens, they saw a continued need for the tactile satisfaction and permanence of printed materials. With a commitment to quality and service, they established DPP, offering a haven for those seeking professional printing solutions.

Over the decades, DPP has grown alongside the evolving needs of its customers. From the initial focus on traditional offset printing, we seamlessly embraced the digital revolution. Today, our repertoire boasts a diverse range of services, catering to both small businesses and large corporations.
