Our Legacy of Excellence

When the Company made a decision to do in-house printing, primarily with the aim of producing stationery for its shipping business, it marked the beginning of a remarkable journey. Since its arrival in Colombo in the early sixties, DPP Printing has managed to be the leader in commercial printing.

The organization quickly expanded beyond just printing stationery and moved into publication printing for both the private and government sector. The Company also enhanced its product range by starting a packaging business, catering to state-controlled exports of the country's chief commodity, tea.

Continuous Growth

Expansion and diversifications along with a host of different value additions have enabled the organization to offer print and packaging on par with global standards. This, in turn, has paved the way for DPP Printing to earn a reputation as the most technically sound and well-equipped printer in the country.

Today, DPP Printing is the preferred printer/print packaging partner for most leading brands locally and globally. With this growth, the Company increased its capacity by over 150% and even today it is the market leader in quality, value additions, and lead times.

Our Management

Our management team comprises technically qualified professionals across all functions and processes who have many years of industry experience & exposure.

With dynamic logistical systems and willingness to incorporate modern business practices, our leadership team ensures DPP Printing remains at the forefront of the printing industry.

The organization has always been committed to sustainable practices and this is exemplified through practices such as harvesting daylight, rooftop solar power generation, harvesting rainwater, and numerous other environmentally conscious initiatives.

Global Reach

The organization caters to many renowned global brands in the USA, UK, and Europe, while simultaneously maintaining its dominance and leadership in the local market.

DPP Printing turned a rural village into a global village with many world-leading brands patronizing the plant with regular business. Factors that keep DPP Printing going strong include our commitment to quality, value additions, and lead times.
